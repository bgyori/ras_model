Growth factors activate EGFR, ERBB2 and FGFR1-4.
Growth factors activate PDGFRA and PDGFRB.
Growth factors activate MET, ROS1 and ALK.
MET, ROS1 and ALK activate SOS1, SOS2/GRB2/SHC1-4 and RASGRF2.
EGFR, ERBB2, PDGFRA, PDGFRB, MET, ROS1, ALK, FGFR1-4 activate SOS1, SOS2/GRB2/SHC1-4 and RASGRF2.
PDGFRA and PDGFRB activate SOS1, SOS2/GRB2/SHC1-4 and RASGRF2.
SOS1, SOS2/GRB2/SHC1-4 and RASGRF2 activate HRAS, NRAS and KRAS.
RASGRP1-4 activates HRAS, KRAS and NRAS.
SPRY1-4 deactivates HRAS, KRAS and NRAS.
RASA1-3/ARHGAP35, RASAL1-3 and NF1.SPRED1-3 deactivate HRAS, NRAS and KRAS.

HRAS, NRAS and KRAS activate RALGDS.
RALGDS activates RALA and RALB.

HRAS, NRAS and KRAS activate ARAF, BRAF and RAF1.
ARAF, BRAF and RAF1 activate MAP2K1 and MAP2K2.
MAP2K1 and MAP2K2 activate MAPK1 and MAPK3.
MAPK1 and MAPK3 activate ETS1-2, FOS and JUN.

KSR1-2 binds ARAF, BRAF and RAF1.
KSR1-2 binds MAP2K1 and MAP2K2.
KSR1-2 binds MAPK1 and MAPK3.

ETS1-2, FOS and JUN activate MDM2, CCND1 and DUSP1-6.
MDM2 deactivates TP53.
CCND1 activates CDK4 and CDK6, which deactivate RB1.
DUSP1-6 deactivates MAPK1 and MAPK3.

SOS1, SOS2/GRB2/SHC1-4 and RASGRF2 activate RHOA, RHOB and RHOC.
RHOA, RHOB and RHOC activate ROCK1 and ROCK2.
RASA1-3/ARHGAP35, RASAL1-3 and NF1/SPRED1-3 deactivate RHOA, RHOB and RHOC.

HRAS, NRAS and KRAS activate PIK3CA-D and PIK3R1-6.
PIK3CA-D and PIK3R1-6 activate PIP3.
PTEN deactivates PIP3.
PIP3 activates PDPk1, AKT1-3 and TIAM1-2.
PDPK1 activates AKT1-3.
AKT1-3 deactivates TSC1/TSC2.
TSC1/TSC2 deactivates RHEB.
RHEB activates MTOR/RPTOR/MLST8.
STK11 activates PRKAA1-2, PRKAB1-2 and PRKAG1-3.
PRKAA1-2, PRKAB1-2 and PRKAG1-3 deactivate MTOR/RPTOR/MLST8.
MTOR/RPTOR/MLST8 deactivates EIF4EBP1.
MTOR/RPTOR/MLST8 activates RPS6KA1-6.
TIAM1-2 activates RAC1-3, which activates PAK1-4.
